Lumolight v5.1

This major update introduces a completely redesigned experience along with several powerful new features:

- Full app redesign
- Create multiple flash profiles and activate them with a single tap
- Greater flexibility for color and duration selection
- Expanded language support
- Performance improvements and bug fixes

About 90% of the app’s code is written directly by the developer (BitMavrick). The project is primarily handcrafted rather than AI-generated.
